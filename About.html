About Us

Founded in 2024 in New York City, The Programming Net is a non-profit organization dedicated to connecting programmers from all over the world. Our mission is to create a platform where developers can share their knowledge, experiences, and ideas, and to connect with like-minded individuals.

Our Founder

Yousif Jawad, born and raised in Brooklyn NY, is a passionate programmer who loves to learn, work, and experiment. Throughout his years in college, Yousif always wanted to make things easier for coders of any skill level to be able to work together on exciting projects
